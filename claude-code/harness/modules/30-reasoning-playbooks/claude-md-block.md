### Reasoning playbooks (30-reasoning-playbooks)

Opt-in reasoning playbooks (bug-investigation, data-analysis, ticket-scoping). Record
progress as `SLOT:` lines under a `## Playbook slots (<shape>)` section in the standing
brief (or its brief-free fallback stub). The escalation ladder degrades to "strongest
available seat, never self-review" when a step's assigned seat isn't available. Full
detail: `docs/orchestrator-playbooks/README.md` (the playbooks themselves are the detail
doc for this module).
